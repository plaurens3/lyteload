LyteLoad for your business.


LyteLoad is a flexible delivery service solution for retailers with oversized items such as furniture, fish tanks, and outdoor equiptment. We offer various partnership agreements that can be tailored to suit your business's needs for providing
delivery, installation, and returns.

The 21st Century Delivery Fleet Alternative

LyteLoad is the perfect tool to take stress off of your business's exisiting delivery fleet and reduce overhead costs.

The sharing economy is unlocking previously untapped capacity, but the retail industry has yet to adapt. Gig Economy platforms are being increasingly accepted by full-time and part-time employees, with over 36% of Americans taking up a side-hustle. One underserved group of side hustlers is the pickup truck owners. Unable to sign up for ridesharing apps or find realiable work on their own, we believe a retail delivery platform will be able to target this demographic to collaborate with local businesses.

Why LyteLoad?

In the crowded tech space, we understand that there are alternatives to LyteLoad's platform, however we pride ourselves on three key aspects of our business:

1. Retail partnerships stregnthen our community by increasing demand in local businesses 


2. A thouroughly screened and trained delivery fleet is crucial in improving the customer experience


3. Flexible plans enable LyteLoad to seamlesssly integrate into your business - we're here to support you not change you
