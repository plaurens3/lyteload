Our Driver Screening Process:

Because transparency matters.

1) Background Check

Anyone who wants to signup needs to pass a background check before moving on to the next step. We immediately decline applicants with any criminal history.

2) Review of Driving History

Driving backround checks are done to make sure they have a clean record and can comfortably drive their larger vehicle even in the city.

3) Mandatory Interview

We have created an onboarding program that consists of videos, guides, and a scenario-based exam that helps prepare drivers and helpers get the job done right.

4) Training & Online Test

All applicants will be interviewed by one of our founders to determine if their past experience, customer service skills and personality are a fit for LyteLoad.

5) Insurance Review

We then confirm the driver has current vehicle records including insurance and state inspections are listed in their name.

6) Regular Account Reviews

We track customer reviews to ensure all of our drivers are up to LyteLoad's standards. If a driver receives less than 4 stars, they are reviewed by a member of our support team.
